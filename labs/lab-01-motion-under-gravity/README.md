# Lab 1: Motion Under Gravity

## Overview

This lab used MATLAB to model the vertical motion of a ball under gravity with a time-step approach. The model generated a height-versus-time signal and was used to determine the maximum height, the time to maximum height, and the estimated time at which the ball returned to the ground.

## Concepts Applied

- Continuous physical motion represented by sampled data
- Time vectors and numerical resolution
- Kinematic modelling
- Signal plotting and interpretation
- Maximum-value and zero-crossing estimation
