# Probability Distributions - Lab Summary

## Aim

To explore the Gaussian distribution computationally and verify the relationship between its standard deviation and full width at half maximum.

## Method Summary

A Gaussian distribution with specified mean and standard deviation is evaluated over a dense range of values. The theoretical FWHM is calculated as

```text
FWHM = 2*sigma*sqrt(2*ln(2))
```

and compared with the width measured at half of the sampled peak value.

## Learning Outcome

The lab connected an analytical probability model with numerical estimation and visual evidence. It highlighted the effect of sampling resolution on measured quantities.
